My Goals for Creating a Good Website

  • Visual Hierarchy
    • Without a visual hierarchy, the webpage may look confusing with various elements and bodies of text and most likely distract the users.
  • Typography
    • It’s best if you stick to 2 typefaces that are legible enough for users to skim through effortlessly.
  • Simplicity or Less is More
    • Speaking of skimming, websites’ bodies of text should be succinct and have attractive typefaces since people have really short attention spans.
  • Accessibility
    • A good website provides usability for people with physical difficulties like visual, motor, and auditory disabilities.
  • Interactive Design.
    • Like its name, an interactive website provides interaction with users. An interactive design is often engages and communicates with them by using several interactive elements like the one of the websites I provided below.
